----BREAKPOINTS
AFTER THE CERTAIN BREAKPOINT STYLE WILL BEHAVE IN CERATIN WAY

EX: xl, lg, md, sm, etc...

----GRID
COLUMN ARE INSERTED IN ROW FOR GRID SYSTEM
A ROW CONSIST OF 12 UNITS  AND ALSO SPECIFC VALUE

EX: col-1, col-6 etc...

WE CAN APPLY SPACING USING BREAKPOINT(CONDITIONAL)
EX: col-xl-1, col-lg-8 col-md-6 etc...

----ALIGNMENT
ALIGN-SELF FOR COLUMN
EX: align-self-start, align-self-end, etc...


ALIGN-ITEMSF, JUSTIFY-CONTENT FOR ROW

verticle alignment
EX: align-item-start, align-item-end 

horigontal alignment
EX: justify-content-start, justify-content-end 

----GUTTERS :USED FOR GIVING PADDING BETWEEN COLUM CONTENTS

EX: g-3

along x-axix
EX: gx-5, gx-3

along y-axis
EX: gy-3, gy-5

MARGIN:

EX: m-3

along x-axix
EX: mx-3 

along y-axis
EX: my-3

PADDING

EX: p-3

along x-axix
EX: px-3 

along y-axis
EX: py-3
